Assignments

Checkpoint One

Exploratory Research

Our Team was tasked with building a Mac OS application. Given the open broadness of this task, we considered the benefits that a Mac has over other devices such as Iphones or Ipads. We looked at some of our favorite apps and why certain apps are best for Mac OS. This document is a look into our exploration and inspiration for our idea to make a study app.

Project Plan

Our project plan involved scheduling key dates, getting organized, and setting goals in order to develop the best Flashcard app possible in the time frame of one semester.

PR / FAQ

Our Press Release and Frequently Asked Questions Document forced us to consider the key aspects of our project. Anticipating questions that users might ask helped us to think through out app from a different perspective.
Assignments

Checkpoint Two

User Research

Our teams research and findings encompassed an array of data collected from surveys, in person interviews, and online reviews of other study apps. This research guided our ultimate product and helped us focus on what was missing in the market.

UX Map

Our UX Map provided us with a comprehensive design of every view we needed to build out. By thinking through the user expierence, we were able to quickly redesign and iterate using the figma program before having to build anything with code.
